Corporate Identity

A bioinformatics partner engineered in Pune.

RASA Life Science Informatics combines computational biology, chemical informatics, and scalable cloud infrastructure to support genomics, transcriptomics, and in-silico drug discovery pipelines for pharma R&D groups, clinical CROs, and academic institutions worldwide.

Mission

Converting raw biological sequences into actionable R&D insights.

Headquartered in Pune, India, RASA serves a global client base spanning pharmaceutical R&D, biotech startups, clinical labs, and academic research institutions. Our computational biology workflows are designed for the realities of modern drug discovery: high data volume, strict validation requirements, and aggressive timelines.

We have processed multi-terabyte sequencing workflows, analyzed 500+ transcriptomics samples, and supported research across oncology, neuroscience, infectious disease, rare disease, and precision medicine.

Every campaign delivers versioned container models, publication-grade figures, and raw annotated outputs designed for team collaboration and regulatory inspection readiness.
