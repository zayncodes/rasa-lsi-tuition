WGS & WES

Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) profiles the entire genomic sequence (both coding exons and non-coding introns), whereas Whole Exome Sequencing (WES) targets only the protein-coding boundaries where key clinical mutations reside.

We deploy GATK4 best practice filters, variant annotations via Ensembl VEP, ANNOVAR, ClinVar classifications, and gnomAD frequency thresholds.

Spatial Transcriptomics

We support 10x Genomics Visium datasets (fresh frozen and FFPE section sections), Slide-seq, and MERFISH high-resolution coordinates.

Metagenomics & Microbiome

We provide taxonomic classification, functional pathway mapping, antibiotic resistome profiles, and community diversity metrics using Kraken2 and HumAnN3 tools.

Drug Discovery

We deploy AutoDock Vina, Smina, CB-Dock2, and MM-GBSA scoring. Molecular dynamics are simulated using GROMACS and AMBER; ADMET profiling uses pkCSM, SwissADME, and ProTox-II; generative molecule design is powered by REINVENT models.

We validation-score candidate targets by mapping them against open-source patient-derived databases (TCGA, GTEx, OpenTargets) and pathways (STRING, Reactome, KEGG).
